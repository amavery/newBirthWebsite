We Love You Here

What To Expect At New Birth?

At New Birth, you'll be welcomed into a loving, God-fearing environment by people who are excited to see you. We want you to have an idea of what to expect when you come to see us. All you have to do from here is come as you are and find out more for yourself!

Dress/Attire?

Here, we just want you to show up! Although we prefer that no offensive or sexually provocative attire is worn, we have no set dress code.

At a Sunday Morning service you can expect:

  • Each service to last about and hour and a half
  • Very high-energy worship music (contemporary and traditional)
  • Passionate and involved preaching
  • Though provoking and convicting teaching from the Bible

We'll help you get to your next step through:

  • Discipleship Coaches to do life with those who are new to the faith
  • Connection Partners that will help you serve in the church and be accountability for you
  • Small Groups that will keep it REAL with themselves and you as it pertains to the faith
